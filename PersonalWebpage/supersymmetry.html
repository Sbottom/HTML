Overview of SUSY

Many parameters means searches need to be model independent to ensure the greatest search sensitivity. Supersymmetry searches therefore look for common signatures. Jets + MET.

The largest branching fracion of Supersymmetric decays involve hadronic decays, hence searches for final states rich in jets are particularly sensitive to supersymmetry. There are however several Standard Model processes that can mimic the event topology of jet-rich events with large missing energy.

The largest background to the analysis is QCD multijet production in association with large missing energy from 'real' sources such as decays to neutrinos or 'fake' sources arising from detector effects. The αT variable is designed to effective exclude events with small or fake MET.
