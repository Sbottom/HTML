Mark Baber


I am a postgraduate researcher working with the Imperial HEP group in searches for supersymmetry with the CMS experiment, particularly in hadronic final states. My work also includes upgrade studies for the Level-1 (L1) Trigger developing jet reconstruction algorithms for the proposed calorimeter upgrade, ready for the resumption of collisions at the LHC in 2015.

Although the current best model of our Universe at the smallest scales, the Standard Model, has been extremely successful it still has several failings. Its inability to provide a candidate for dark matter and unify the force coupling constants at high energies are amongst the problems that are sought to be resolved by a more complete theory.
